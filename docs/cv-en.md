### Norwegian Tax Administration (Skatteetaten) — Skills Registration System
**Role:** Solution Architect / Developer
**Period:** Feb 2025 – Jan 2026

Initiated to solve the lack of visibility into which competencies employees across Skatteetaten's IT departments hold. Existing data was fragmented across Excel files and SharePoint lists, creating inaccuracies and making it difficult for managers to keep information up to date.

**Deliverables:**
- Fully access-controlled canvas app for registering and maintaining employee competencies
- Centralised data model in Dataverse, replacing fragmented Excel and SharePoint sources
- Automated daily data flows pulling HR extracts from SharePoint into Dataverse
- Role-based access control using Azure (Entra) security groups and Dataverse roles — managers see only their own employees
- Power BI integration for cross-departmental analytics and reporting
- Automated email alerts to managers when employees are missing required competencies
- UI/UX design, documentation, and knowledge transfer to internal teams

**Technology:** Power Apps (Canvas), Dataverse, Azure Entra ID, Power Automate, Power BI, SharePoint
